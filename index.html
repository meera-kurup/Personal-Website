United States Senate Youth Program NH Delegate

The Hearst Foundation and United States Senate

Delegate and Scholar representing New Hampshire for the The United States Senate Youth Program (USSYP), an intensive week-long educational experience and scholarship sponsored by the United States Senate for outstanding high school students who are interested in pursuing careers in public service. The 104 student delegates attend briefings and meetings with Senators, the President, a Justice of the Supreme Court, cabinet members, federal agency leaders and senior members of the national media.
